Rules for Survival

1. A set of three levels will be unlocked every midnight.

2. Every level have multiple questions. One of them will be allocated to each user.

3. For every level maximum possible score is 100(except 10 th Level).

4. Scoring at each level has following rules :

 Every wrong attempt leads to a penalty of 1 marks.

 On a successful submission of a user the maximum possible marks for the corresponding question will be deducted by 5(it will not make any impact on other questions of that level).

 Time taken by the user will be considered from the time of unlocking the level to the time of first successful submission.

 Wrong attempts will be considered only before first successful submission.

 There is minimum threshold score for each level (Penalty will be considered explicitly for final scoring).
